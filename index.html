Mark Knopfler is a British singer, songwriter and guitarist. He is most known for being the lead singer and co-founder of Dire Straits with his brother David Knopfler. The Rolling Stones named him one of the "100 Greatest Guitarists of All Time" and holds four Grammy Awards. Many people consider Mark's style to be unique because instead of using a pick he uses his fingers to pluck the strings of the guitar. This is a difficult style to master as your fingers can become blistered and cross picking (picking the wrong string) can occur. Knopfler's unique sound blends traditional Rock music with influences of: Blues, Country, and Celtic music. As of January 2020, Mark Knopfler has sold over 120 million records between his personal work and Dire Straits.
